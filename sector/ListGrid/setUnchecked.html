Sets the element at the given x and y to value.

The x and y coordinates are zero-based, where x is the horizontal axis and y is the vertical axis. The origin (0, 0) is at the top-left corner of the grid.

If the coordinates are out of bounds, the behavior is undefined.

Warning

Use this method with caution, as it may lead to undefined behavior if the coordinates are out of bounds. It is recommended to use set instead.
